This is project was built with Typescript, React, NextJS, and MongoDB.

## Why TimeReq
Well the idea was pretty simple. If someone schedules time on your calendar with something like Calendly, then it's just there and you have to deal with that time not being open to events you actually want to attend or you are double booked.

Well, I didn't like that, so TimeReq was born.

Here are the benefits:

- I'm aren't paying a subscription to another scheduling tool.
- People aren't forcing meetings on me.
- I actually get to decide whether I want to even meet with someone or not.

If this tool works well enough, I will productize it for other people to use for a small fee.
